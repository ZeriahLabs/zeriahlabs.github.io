MISSION BRIEFING

OBJECTIVE: OPTIMIZE BATTERY

Your goal is to get your Battery Power closer to 21 than the Enemy, without overloading (going over 21).

THE CORE LOGIC

  • ⚠️ IF ENEMY IS STRONG (7, 8, 9, 10, A): They will likely get a high score. You must risk it and CHARGE (Hit) until you have at least 17.
  • 🛡️ IF ENEMY IS WEAK (2, 3, 4, 5, 6): They will likely overload (bust). Play it safe! SHIELD (Stand) on anything 12 or higher.

VARIABLE DETECTED: THE "ACE"

If you see an Ace (A), your battery is flexible (Soft). It counts as 1 or 11.

Rule of thumb: Always HIT a Soft 17 or lower. You can't bust!
