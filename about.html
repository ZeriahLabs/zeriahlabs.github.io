Zeriah Labs

Zeriah Labs was created with a simple idea: to provide quality, educational, and enjoyable content for kids. Instead of endless videos or cartoons, we design experiences that are engaging, cute, and meaningful — something that entertains while helping children learn.

As parents, we place privacy and security at the center of everything we build. Fun and learning come next, supported by tools that give parents helpful feedback along the way.

Our goal is to create apps that are safe, educational, and delightful — evolving over time to meet the needs of families while staying rooted in care and trust.
